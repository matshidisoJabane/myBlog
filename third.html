Kinky Hair

Bold, beautiful, and naturally voluminous, the kinky curl is made up of thousands of z-shaped strands. Defined by a close-knit curl pattern and luxurious volume, kinky curls wind up and out. Yet the tighter the curl, the more difficult it is for oil from the scalp to travel the length of the strand and moisturize.
